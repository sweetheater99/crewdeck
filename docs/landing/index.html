Without Crewdeck

  • ✗ One failure kills the chain — you don't notice until hours later
  • ✗ No idea what anything costs until the invoice arrives
  • ✗ Context switching between dashboards, logs, and chat windows

With Crewdeck

  • ✓ Define task chains with dependencies — agents hand off automatically
  • ✓ Failures retry, reassign, or escalate to you — nothing silently dies
  • ✓ Real-time cost tracking per agent, per task, per day
  • ✓ One dashboard. Morning digest on Telegram. Act from your phone.
